SKARDU

Skardu, located in the Gilgit-Baltistan region of northern Pakistan, is a breathtaking valley surrounded by the towering peaks of the Karakoram Range. Known as a gateway to some of the world's highest mountains, including K2, Skardu is famous for its stunning landscapes, crystal-clear lakes, and rich cultural heritage. The serene beauty of places like Shangrila Lake, Deosai National Park, and the ancient Skardu Fort make it a popular destination for adventurers, trekkers, and nature lovers alike.

GILGIT

Gilgit, the capital of Gilgit-Baltistan in Pakistan, is a vibrant town surrounded by towering mountains and lush valleys. It serves as a crucial gateway for trekkers and adventurers heading to the majestic Karakoram Range, including K2, the second-highest mountain in the world. Gilgit boasts a rich history, having been a significant stop along ancient trade routes like the Silk Road. The region is known for its diverse culture, breathtaking landscapes, and warm hospitality.

HUNZA

Hunza, a picturesque valley in Gilgit-Baltistan, Pakistan, is renowned for its stunning natural beauty, rich history, and vibrant culture. Nestled in the Karakoram mountain range, it is surrounded by snow-capped peaks, including the iconic Rakaposhi. Hunza is famous for its lush green terraced fields, ancient forts like Baltit and Altit, and the warm hospitality of its people. Known for its healthy lifestyle, Hunza is often referred to as a 'paradise on earth'
